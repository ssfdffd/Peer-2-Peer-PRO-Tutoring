Whiteboard Tutorial

Learn how to use all the features of our interactive whiteboard

Drawing Tools

Use pen, highlighter, and eraser tools with adjustable sizes and colors.

Shapes & Lines

Insert rectangles, circles, triangles, arrows, stars, and custom shapes.

Text Editing

Add text with customizable fonts, sizes, colors, and styles (bold, italic, underline).

Media Insertion

Insert images, documents (PDF, Word, PowerPoint), and videos directly onto the board.

Audio Notes

Record voice explanations and attach them to specific areas of your whiteboard.

Save & Export

Save your work in multiple formats (JSON, PNG, JPEG, SVG) and export for sharing.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use right-click on any object for quick actions (delete, copy, etc.)
  • Double-click on text to edit it
  • Hold Shift while drawing to create straight lines or perfect shapes
  • Use Ctrl + mouse wheel to zoom in/out quickly
  • Your work is auto-saved every 30 seconds
  • Use multiple pages for different topics or lessons
  • Export as PNG for high-quality images to share with students
  • Attach audio recordings to explain complex diagrams
